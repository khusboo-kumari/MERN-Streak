 Exercise 01
  -----------
  Highlight all of the words over 8 characters long in the paragraph text (with a yellow background for example) .  


Solution : 
To highlight all the words over 8 characters long in the paragraph text using the DOM in JavaScript, 
you can follow these steps:

Retrieve the paragraph text from the HTML.
Split the text into individual words.
Check the length of each word.
If a word is longer than 8 characters, wrap it in a span with a yellow background.
Replace the original paragraph content with the modified one .
